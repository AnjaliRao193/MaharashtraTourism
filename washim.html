Must-Visit Attractions

Padmatirtha Lake Temple

About Washim

Washim, a picturesque district in Maharashtra, is known for its rich history, serene temples, and natural beauty. With attractions like Padmatirtha Lake Temple, it offers a perfect blend of culture and tranquility.
