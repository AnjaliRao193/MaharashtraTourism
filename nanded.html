About Nanded

"Nanded, a city of spiritual significance, is home to the revered Hazur Sahib Gurudwara, a sacred pilgrimage site. Known for its historical forts, serene temples, and peaceful riversides, Nanded offers a perfect blend of culture, spirituality, and natural beauty. With places like Kandhar Fort and the mahur, Nanded is a must-visit destination for history lovers and spiritual seekers alike."
