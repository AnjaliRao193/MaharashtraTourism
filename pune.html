About Pune

Pune, the "Cultural Capital of Maharashtra," is a vibrant city where history meets modernity. Known for its rich Maratha heritage, iconic forts, and spiritual landmarks, it’s also a hub for education, IT, and innovation. From the serene Aga Khan Palace to the bustling streets around Shaniwar Wada and the tranquil vibes of Sinhagad Fort, Pune offers a perfect blend of tradition and urban charm. Whether you're a history buff, foodie, or adventure seeker, Pune’s diverse attractions promise an unforgettable experience!
